About Mediacast1.ai

Mediacast1.ai is a pioneering AI-driven broadcasting platform that revolutionizes streaming workflows for independent podcasters, professional broadcasters, creators, and enterprise-level studios alike. With roots in the early internet broadcasting era, we’ve fused legacy SHOUTcast/Icecast paradigms with modern AI automation and streaming cloud infrastructure to empower your media like never before.

Features & Tools

  • 🔊 AI-Powered Broadcast Automation: Smart scheduling, voiceovers, and playlist generation.
  • 📡 Next-Gen Icecast2 Fork: Golang-rewritten ultra-low-latency server for audio/video streams.
  • 📺 Cross-Platform Streaming: Integrated support for YouTube, Twitch, Facebook, and custom RTMP endpoints.
  • 📈 Analytics Suite: Real-time listener/viewer tracking, sentiment analysis, and engagement scoring.
  • 💾 PlaylistGen AI: Import/export .m3u/.pls/.json playlists, format with correct ID3 metadata.
  • 🎙️ Creator Tools: Podcast editing, show planning, AI-driven enhancements.
  • 🔐 Secure & Scalable: CDN-ready, HTTPS streaming, GitHub Pages integration, and open-source friendly.
